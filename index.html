The Process

Four unhurried steps.

  1. 01

    Consult

    A relaxed conversation in your home. We bring samples, you walk us through the rooms, and we talk about light, privacy, and how you actually live.

  2. 02

    Design & Measure

    We measure to a sixteenth of an inch and sketch your options on site. You get a full written quote before we leave — no pressure, no waiting.

  3. 03

    Craft

    Your treatments are built to your exact specifications by trusted American workrooms and partners. Most orders ship in 2–4 weeks.

  4. 04

    Install

    We install carefully, demonstrate every motor and remote, and don't leave until every window is perfect.
