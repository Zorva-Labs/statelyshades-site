Common Questions

What people ask us.

What window treatments does Stately Shades install?

We install custom plantation and wood shutters, motorized and smart shades, cellular and honeycomb shades, fabric sheers with floating vanes, zebra and banded shades, woven wood and bamboo naturals, roller and solar shades, Roman shades, blackout drapery, custom valances and cornices, and outdoor shades, retractable screens, and retractable awnings.

Do you work on new home construction?

Yes. We outfit new builds with custom plantation shutters, motorized smart shades, and complete whole-home window-treatment packages. We coordinate with builders and homeowners directly.

Can you replace the builder-grade blinds I inherited?

Absolutely — that's one of our most common projects. We swap tired builder-grade blinds for custom plantation shutters, motorized shades, cellular honeycombs, woven naturals, or modern fabrics, one room at a time or the whole house at once.

Do you have affordable blinds? What is the cheapest option?

Yes — our most affordable option is custom-fit faux wood blinds. Faux wood is humidity-proof, child-safe cordless, available in 2″ and 2.5″ slats, and looks like real wood for a fraction of the cost. We offer whole-house packages with bulk pricing for builders, landlords, and first-home buyers, and the same measure-and-install care that goes into our heirloom shutters. Excellent for kitchens, bathrooms, kids' rooms, basements, rental properties, and any value-conscious project. We'll quote your exact windows in your home — no generic online estimates.

Will you install blinds I bought from Lowes, Home Depot, Costco, or Blinds.com?

Yes — we offer install-only service across Middle Tennessee for any brand and any product. Bring us blinds, shutters, or shades you ordered from Lowes, Home Depot, Costco, Blinds.com, Select Blinds, Amazon, IKEA, or anywhere else — and we'll measure twice, mount level, demonstrate the mechanism, and haul away the packaging. Flat-rate per window. Every install carries our workmanship guarantee.

How much does install-only service cost per window?

Install-only service runs $45–$85 per window for standard blinds and shades, $65–$140 per window for plantation shutters, and $95–$180 per shade for motorized treatments (programming included). Specialty installs — sliding-door tracks, French-door mounts, arched and skylight shades, drapery rods — are quoted on the visit. A typical 8-window install-only job lands between $360 and $720 installed.

Do you offer blind repair, shutter repair, and shade repair?

Yes — we fix broken blinds, shutters, and shades of any brand: Hunter Douglas, Norman, Somfy, Graber, Levolor, Bali, Lutron, and builder-grade. Tilt rods, lift cords, broken slats, frayed cords, missing wands, stuck motors, blind restringing, PowerView motor service, blackout liner replacement, shutter louver repair. Often faster and more economical than full replacement.

How much does blind repair cost near me in Middle Tennessee?

Most blind repairs (restring, tilt-rod replacement, broken slat swap) run $75–$200 per blind plus parts, and we always quote in person before any work. Motor service for Hunter Douglas PowerView or Somfy hardware is typically $150–$300 depending on what needs replacing. We come to you anywhere within our 90-mile service radius — no trip charge.

Is the in-home consultation really free?

Yes — no charge for the consultation, no travel fee within our 90-mile service area, and no obligation. We bring physical samples, measure precisely, sketch options on site, and leave you with a written quote.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Middle Tennessee region within a 90-mile radius of Gallatin — Nashville, Hendersonville, Franklin, Brentwood, Mount Juliet, Lebanon, Goodlettsville, Murfreesboro, Springfield, Clarksville, Cookeville, Smyrna, Spring Hill, and into Bowling Green, Kentucky.

Are you a Hunter Douglas dealer?

Yes. We're an independent dealer of Hunter Douglas, plus Norman, Somfy, Graber, Sol-Lux, and Specialized Shading Systems. Being independent means we specify the right product from the best manufacturer for every window — not just one brand.

Do your motorized shades work with Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit?

Yes. Every motorized shade we install integrates with the full home-automation ecosystem — Amazon Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit, Lutron, and Control4. Schedule scenes by time of day, track the sun across rooms, or open every shade with a single voice command.

How long do custom orders typically take?

Most custom window treatments ship from our manufacturing partners within two to four weeks of order placement. Larger whole-home projects may take longer depending on product mix and customizations.

Is financing available?

Yes — flexible financing is available on most projects. We discuss options during the in-home consultation when we present your written quote.

How much do plantation shutters cost?

Custom plantation shutters typically range from $250 to $450 per window depending on size, material (hardwood vs composite), louver width, and finish. We give you a precise written quote in your home — no generic online estimates that miss the mark on your actual windows.

What are the best blinds for bedrooms?

For bedrooms we typically recommend blackout cellular shades, room-darkening Roman shades, or plantation shutters with full louver closure. Top-down / bottom-up cellulars are popular in nurseries and primary bedrooms — darken the lower half for privacy while keeping morning light coming in from the top.

Can you motorize my existing blinds?

In some cases yes — Somfy and similar motors can retrofit onto certain existing roller shades and Romans. For older blinds with broken cords or aging hardware, replacing with new motorized window treatments is usually more reliable and not much more expensive than a retrofit.

What window treatments work for sliding glass doors and French doors?

Sliding glass doors: panel-track systems, Hunter Douglas Luminette® vertical privacy sheers, or wide-vane vertical blinds. French doors: plantation shutters with hinged panels, door-mount cellular shades, or motorized roller shades that mount above the door frame.

Are your blinds child-safe and pet-safe?

Yes. Every blind, shade, and shutter we install is cordless or motorized by default for child and pet safety, meeting the Window Covering Manufacturers Association safety standard. For homes with kids we typically recommend motorization, which removes cords entirely.
