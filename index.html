Philosophy

Three things matter to us:

The Process

Four unhurried steps.

  1. i

    Consult

    A relaxed conversation in your home. We bring samples, walk the rooms with you, and talk about light, privacy, and how you actually live.

  2. ii

    Measure

    We measure to a sixteenth of an inch and sketch your options on site. You get a written quote before we leave — no pressure, no waiting.

  3. iii

    Craft

    Your treatments are built to exact specification by our manufacturing partners — Hunter Douglas, Norman, Somfy, Graber, Sol-Lux, and others. Most orders ship within two to four weeks.

  4. iv

    Install

    We install carefully, demonstrate every motor and remote, and don't leave until every window is right. Flexible financing available on most projects.

The right window treatment disappears into the room — until you notice the room finally feels finished.
