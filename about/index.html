A Family Business · Gallatin, TN

We make windows feel like home.

Stately Shades is a family-owned, family-run custom window treatment company serving Gallatin, Nashville, and a 90-mile radius across Middle Tennessee — built on tight measurements, honest pricing, and the kind of communication you don't usually get from a contractor.

Years of windows, one family.

We're the Blair family, and Stately Shades is what we built when we got tired of seeing beautiful homes saddled with builder-grade blinds, broken cords, and lopsided shades that never quite fit. So we started a business that does the opposite — custom plantation shutters, motorized shades, cellular blinds, woven wood, sheer vanes, and Hunter Douglas window treatments measured to a sixteenth of an inch and installed cleanly the first time.

We're based in Gallatin, Tennessee, and we cover everything inside a roughly 90-mile radius — Nashville, Hendersonville, Franklin, Brentwood, Mt. Juliet, Lebanon, Murfreesboro, Springfield, Clarksville, Cookeville, Spring Hill, Bowling Green KY, and every town in between. No travel charge. No consultation fee. We bring the showroom to your living room.

Working with us looks like this: a relaxed in-home consult, a written quote before we leave, no high-pressure pitch. Reasonable pricing, transparent options at three different price points, and a real human you can text when you have a question. Same person who quoted the job is the person who installs it.

We've spent years doing this work, and the relationships we've built reflect it — we're the go-to window treatment installer for real estate agents staging listings, general contractors finishing builds, interior designers sourcing for clients, and apartment complex managers outfitting whole properties at once. When professionals need it done right and on schedule, they call us.
